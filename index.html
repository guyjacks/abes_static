Our Mission

Abe’s Kitchen came to life at the Columbus Give Back Hack 2015. The team formed a plan added seed capital and began to develop menus and the needed supply chain. The first meal was prepared on Labor Day and served outside Linden Stem school.
The Fall season continued with meals served three days a week until late October. Meals were sold for $2 (or 3 for $5) and were based off of the H.E.A.T Healthy Economical and Tasty concept and were well received by customers. Meals will start again in the Spring through the Fall and should expand to multiple trucks and feeding sites.
